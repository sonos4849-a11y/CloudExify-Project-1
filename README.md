# CloudExify-Project-1
I have performed sales dataanalysis at Cloud exify intership
### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

#### a. Sales by Product Line
- The total sales were aggregated by `PRODUCTLINE`. The top product lines by sales are:
  - Classic Cars
  - Vintage Cars
  - Motorcycles
  - Trucks and Buses
  - Planes
  - Ships
  - Trains

#### b. Sales by Deal Size
- A bar chart was generated to visualize total sales based on `DEALSIZE`.
- This plot helps in understanding which deal sizes contribute most to the overall sales revenue.

#### c. Monthly Sales Trend
- The `ORDERDATE` column was converted to datetime objects.
- A line plot showed the monthly sales trend over the period covered by the dataset. This helps identify any seasonality or general trends in sales performance.

#### d. Sales Distribution by Country
- A pie chart was generated to display the proportion of sales from different countries.
- This visualization highlights the key geographical markets for the business, with Spain, France, and Australia appearing as significant contributors among the countries with complete address data.
